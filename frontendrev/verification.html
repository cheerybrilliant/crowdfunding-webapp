Patient Verification

Welcome! To ensure the authenticity of patient campaigns, we require verification. Please upload the following documents for hospital review.
Please upload a clear photo of yourself
Record a short video (30-60 seconds) introducing yourself and explaining your situation
You can upload medical reports, diagnosis letters, or hospital bills
Important: Uploaded media helps hospitals review and verify patient requests. Your information will be kept confidential and only used for verification purposes. Once verified, you can create your fundraising campaign.

What Happens Next?

  1. Your documents will be reviewed by our partner hospitals
  2. Verification typically takes 2-3 business days
  3. You will receive an email notification once verified
  4. After verification, you can create your fundraising campaign
