How CancerCare Works

Simple, transparent, and secure fundraising for cancer patients

1

Patient Verification

Cancer patients register and submit medical documents to our partner hospitals for verification.

2

Create Campaign

Verified patients create fundraising campaigns with their story and treatment needs.

3

Community Support

Donors browse verified campaigns and contribute through secure mobile money payments.

4

Get Treatment

Funds are used directly for medical treatment, giving patients the care they need.

About CancerCare Platform

Created by six passionate students from Yaoundé, CancerCare is more than just a fundraising platform. We're building a community where cancer patients can find hope, support, and the financial resources they need to fight their battle.

Every patient is verified by our partner hospitals, ensuring that your donations reach those who truly need help. Together, we're making quality cancer treatment accessible to everyone in our community.
