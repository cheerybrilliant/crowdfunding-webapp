Partner Hospitals in Yaoundé

We work with trusted hospitals in Yaoundé to verify patient campaigns and provide quality cancer care. These partner hospitals help ensure that every patient on our platform receives authentic support.

Why Partner Hospitals Matter

Our partner hospitals play a crucial role in verifying patient identities and medical conditions. This verification process ensures that donations reach genuine cancer patients who need support. All hospitals listed here are authorized to verify patients for our platform.

How to Get Verified by a Partner Hospital

  1. Create a patient account on our platform
  2. Complete the verification form with your documents
  3. Visit any of our partner hospitals with your medical records
  4. Hospital staff will review your case and verify your identity
  5. Once verified, you can create your fundraising campaign
