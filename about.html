About Us

Our Mission

A group of six students came together with a shared goal: to help cancer patients by creating a platform for donations, support, and health seminars. We believe that no one should face cancer alone, and that together, we can make a real difference in the lives of patients and their families.

Our platform connects cancer patients with generous donors, organizes educational health seminars, and facilitates medical outreach programs. We are committed to transparency, compassion, and making healthcare support accessible to everyone who needs it.

What We Do

Patient Support

We help cancer patients create verified fundraising campaigns to cover their medical expenses. Every campaign is reviewed and verified by our partner hospitals to ensure authenticity.

Health Education

We organize seminars and workshops about cancer prevention, early detection, and treatment options. Education is key to fighting cancer.

Community Outreach

We facilitate free health screenings and medical gatherings in communities, making healthcare more accessible to those who need it most.

Community Outreach

Our Commitment to Transparency

We take transparency seriously. All donations are managed responsibly and used to support verified patients or organize approved health seminars and medical gatherings. We work closely with partner hospitals in Yaoundé to verify every patient campaign, ensuring that your donations reach those who truly need them.

This platform is in its early stage, and we are continuously working to improve our services. Your feedback and support help us grow and serve the community better.

Our Partner Hospitals in Yaoundé

We are proud to partner with five trusted hospitals in Yaoundé, Cameroon. These medical facilities help us verify patient identities and medical conditions, ensuring the authenticity of every campaign on our platform.

Our partner hospitals include Yaoundé General Hospital, Yaoundé Central Hospital, Yaoundé University Teaching Hospital, Centre Hospitalier d'Essos, and Hôpital de District de Biyem-Assi. Together, we are committed to supporting cancer patients in their fight against this disease.
